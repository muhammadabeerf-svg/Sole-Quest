Who We Are

The Sole Quest Mission

Sole Quest was born on a rainy Sunday long run outside Lincoln in 2024. We were frustrated — the running shoe market felt either too technical and jargon-heavy, or too generic and uninspiring. We wanted something different.

Our philosophy is simple: the right shoe changes everything. Whether you're a first-time 5K runner or a seasoned ultramarathoner, every foot deserves a thoughtfully engineered shoe.

We source from sustainable suppliers, use recycled materials wherever possible, and offset carbon from all UK deliveries. Running is good for you — it should be good for the planet too.
